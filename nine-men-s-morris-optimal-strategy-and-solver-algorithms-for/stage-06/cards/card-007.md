# Digital Twin: Origin to Future Survey

## Cite_Key
singh2021digital

## Problem
Rapid growth in Digital Twin publications requires systematic review to clarify concepts, applications, and distinguish DT from related technologies.

## Method
Comprehensive literature survey tracing DT from conceptual origin through current applications and future directions in Industry 4.0.

## Data
Published literature on Digital Twin implementations across manufacturing, healthcare, smart cities, and other domains.

## Metrics
Qualitative categorization of DT applications, enabling technologies, and maturity levels.

## Findings
DT enables real-time monitoring, optimization, predictive maintenance, and remote access through virtual replicas of physical entities. Clarifies DT definition and distinguishes it from simulation, noting bidirectional real-time data exchange as the key differentiator.

## Limitations
Broad survey scope limits depth on any single domain; rapidly evolving field may outpace review; limited quantitative comparison of DT implementations.

## Citation
Singh et al. (2021). Digital Twin: Origin to Future. Appl Syst Innov. doi:10.3390/asi4020036

# 21st Century Skills: Assessment and Certification of the 4Cs

## Cite_Key
thornhillmiller2023creativity

## Problem
Future-of-work demands 21st century soft skills (creativity, critical thinking, collaboration, communication) but assessment and certification frameworks remain underdeveloped.

## Method
Review of individual-level performance assessment for each of the 4Cs, plus institutional-level systemic support assessment and labelization (official certification) processes.

## Data
Published assessment instruments, certification frameworks, and institutional evaluation approaches for 4C skills.

## Metrics
Individual performance measures and institutional support indicators for creativity, critical thinking, collaboration, and communication.

## Findings
Presents labelization as a publicly trusted assessment and certification mechanism for 21st century skills at the institutional level. Distinguishes individual skill assessment from systemic support measurement. Argues institutional certification can drive skill development at scale.

## Limitations
Cultural variability in skill definitions not fully addressed; labelization adoption remains nascent; long-term impact of certification on actual skill development unvalidated.

## Citation
Thornhill-Miller et al. (2023). Creativity, Critical Thinking, Communication, and Collaboration. J Intelligence. doi:10.3390/jintelligence11030054

# Comprehensive Survey Toward 6G Communications

## Cite_Key
jiang2021road

## Problem
With 5G deployment underway, academia and industry need a forward-looking vision for 6G systems covering drivers, use cases, requirements, and enabling technologies.

## Method
Comprehensive survey synthesizing drivers, use cases, usage scenarios, requirements, KPIs, architecture, and enabling technologies for 6G.

## Data
Published literature on 5G deployments, projected data traffic growth, emerging application requirements.

## Metrics
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for 6G including data rate, latency, reliability, and coverage targets.

## Findings
Identifies explosive data traffic growth and new application demands as key 6G drivers. Maps out potential 6G architecture and enabling technologies. Argues the need for 6G is justified by limitations of 5G for future use cases.

## Limitations
Highly speculative given early stage of 6G research; KPI targets may shift as standardization progresses; technology feasibility not validated experimentally.

## Citation
Jiang et al. (2021). The Road Towards 6G. IEEE Open J Commun Soc. doi:10.1109/ojcoms.2021.3057679

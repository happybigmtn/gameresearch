# Best Practices in Exploratory Factor Analysis

## Cite_Key
costello2020best

## Problem
EFA is a complex multi-step process with many software-dependent choices; researchers lack consolidated practical guidance.

## Method
Methodological review and synthesis of best practices across popular software packages for four key EFA decision points.

## Data
Review of published methodological literature and software documentation for EFA procedures.

## Metrics
Qualitative recommendations for extraction method, rotation method, number of factors, and sample size.

## Findings
Provides consolidated recommendations on (a) extraction methods, (b) rotation approaches, (c) determining number of factors to retain, and (d) adequate sample size for stable EFA solutions.

## Limitations
Recommendations are general-purpose and may not cover all domain-specific scenarios; software implementations may diverge from described defaults over time.

## Citation
Costello & Osborne (2020). Best practices in exploratory factor analysis. doi:10.7275/jyj1-4868

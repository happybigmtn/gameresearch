# Electric Vehicle Adoption: Benefits, Challenges, Solutions

## Cite_Key
alanazi2023electric

## Problem
Rising oil costs and carbon emissions drive EV interest, but infrastructure, range anxiety, charging scarcity, and battery performance hinder widespread adoption.

## Method
Literature review surveying EV evolution, benefits, adoption challenges, and proposed technological and policy solutions.

## Data
Published studies on EV technology, infrastructure deployment, battery performance, and policy interventions.

## Metrics
Carbon emission reduction potential, infrastructure cost estimates, range and charging time benchmarks.

## Findings
EVs significantly reduce carbon emissions and air pollution. Key barriers include high infrastructure costs, sparse charging networks, range anxiety, and battery limitations. Solutions include battery technology advances, expanded charging infrastructure, policy incentives, and smart grid integration.

## Limitations
Review-level evidence without original empirical data; rapidly evolving technology landscape may outdate specific findings; geographic variability in adoption barriers not fully addressed.

## Citation
Alanazi (2023). Electric Vehicles: Benefits, Challenges, and Potential Solutions. Appl Sci. doi:10.3390/app13106016

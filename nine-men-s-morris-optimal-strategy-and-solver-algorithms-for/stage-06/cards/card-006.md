# ESMO Guidelines for Localised Colon Cancer

## Cite_Key
argiles2020localised

## Problem
Need for standardized clinical practice guidelines for diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up of localised colon cancer in Europe.

## Method
Expert consensus clinical practice guidelines developed by ESMO based on systematic evidence review.

## Data
Systematic review of clinical trial data, observational studies, and expert consensus for colon cancer management.

## Metrics
Evidence levels and recommendation grades for diagnostic workup, surgical approaches, adjuvant therapy, and follow-up protocols.

## Findings
Provides comprehensive evidence-based recommendations for staging, surgical management, adjuvant chemotherapy decisions, and surveillance protocols for localised colon cancer.

## Limitations
Abstract not available; guidelines may not fully address resource-limited settings or evolving molecular profiling technologies.

## Citation
Argilés et al. (2020). Localised colon cancer: ESMO Clinical Practice Guidelines. Ann Oncol. doi:10.1016/j.annonc.2020.06.022

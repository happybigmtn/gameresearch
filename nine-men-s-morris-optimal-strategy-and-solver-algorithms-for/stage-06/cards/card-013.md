# Challenges in Deploying Machine Learning Systems

## Cite_Key
paleyes2022challenges

## Problem
ML has moved from research to production, but deployment presents numerous practical challenges across the ML workflow that lack systematic documentation.

## Method
Survey of published case studies and deployment reports across industries, mapping challenges to stages of the ML deployment workflow.

## Data
Published reports of real-world ML deployments across various use cases, industries, and applications.

## Metrics
Qualitative categorization of challenges by deployment workflow stage (data management, model training, testing, deployment, monitoring).

## Findings
Practitioners face issues at every stage of ML deployment. Challenges span data quality, model validation, infrastructure, monitoring, and organizational factors. Lays out a research agenda for addressing deployment challenges systematically.

## Limitations
Relies on published reports which may over-represent successful deployments; rapidly evolving MLOps tooling may address some identified challenges; industry-specific nuances may be underrepresented.

## Citation
Paleyes et al. (2022). Challenges in Deploying Machine Learning. ACM Comput Surv. doi:10.1145/3533378
